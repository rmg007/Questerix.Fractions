About Questerix Fractions

Questerix Fractions is a browser-based math game for children in Kindergarten through Grade 2 (ages 5–8). Players learn fraction concepts — halves, thirds, fourths, and how to compare them — by dragging, snapping, and sorting colorful shapes with a magnetic mechanic that makes equal partitioning feel tactile and intuitive.

There is nothing to install, no account to create, and no data leaves your device. Progress is stored locally in your browser and can be exported as a JSON file at any time. The game is designed for short sessions of 10–15 minutes and works on phones, tablets, and desktops.

The project is in active development (Phase 1 complete) with a research goal: does the magnetic-drag mechanic help K–2 students improve fraction performance compared to a standard paper pre/post test? Levels 1–9 cover the full Grade K–2 CCSS fraction strand.
