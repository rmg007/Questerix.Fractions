Questerix Fractions

Privacy Notice — Last updated April 27, 2026

This app stores everything on your device only. No data is ever sent to any server.

Questerix Fractions is a math learning game designed for children in Kindergarten through Grade 2 (ages 5–8). We take the privacy of young learners very seriously. This notice explains exactly what information the app does and does not collect.

Data Storage — Local Only

All progress, settings, and game data are stored exclusively on this device using your browser's built-in local storage (IndexedDB). This data never leaves the device and is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

  • No accounts or user profiles are created.
  • No login, email address, or password is required.
  • Progress data is private to this device and browser.
  • You can clear all data at any time through the app's Settings screen or your browser settings.

No Personal Information Collected

Questerix Fractions does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII), including but not limited to:

  • Names, ages, or dates of birth
  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Location or IP addresses
  • Photos or voice recordings
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints

No Advertising or Third-Party Trackers

The app contains no advertising of any kind. There are no third-party analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, social-media buttons, or any other technology that could observe your child's usage. The app makes no network requests during normal gameplay.

Designed for Children (COPPA)

Questerix Fractions is designed for children ages 5–8 and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Because we collect no personal information whatsoever — from children or adults — no parental consent mechanism is required. There is nothing to consent to.

No Network Required

Once installed, Questerix Fractions works fully offline. The app does not communicate with any external server during gameplay, progress saving, or any other normal operation.
