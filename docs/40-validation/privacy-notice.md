# Privacy Notice

_Per audit §4.7. Plain-language notice for parents, guardians, and caregivers. The app links to this page from its Settings screen._

---

## What we collect: nothing leaves your device. Period.

Questerix Fractions stores progress data locally on the device you use. No data is ever sent to a server, a third party, or anyone else. There is no internet connection required after the app loads.

---

## What's stored locally

Two kinds of data are saved in your browser's private storage (IndexedDB) on your device:

- **Progress** — which levels your child has played, how many questions they answered, and their current mastery state.
- **Preferences** — settings like audio on/off and reduced-motion mode.

We do not store your child's name, email address, physical location, device ID, or any information that could identify them to us or anyone else.

---

## How to export your data

Tap **Settings → Export Backup** inside the app. This saves a JSON file to your device. You can open it, read it, or keep it as a backup. We never see it.

---

## How to delete your data

Tap **Settings → Reset Device** inside the app. This permanently removes all locally stored progress and preferences from this device.

Alternatively, you can clear all site data from your browser's settings under Site Permissions or Privacy → Clear Browsing Data, then select this app's domain.

---

## Cookies

None. The app does not use cookies of any kind.

---

## Third parties

None. The app does not load analytics tools, advertising scripts, tracking pixels, or fonts from external servers (such as Google Fonts). Everything needed to run the app is bundled and served from the same domain.

---

## Children

This app is designed for children ages 5–8. We deliberately collect no personal data so that COPPA (US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR-K (European data rules for children) simply do not apply — there is nothing to process, store, or share. No consent mechanism is needed because no personal information is collected.
