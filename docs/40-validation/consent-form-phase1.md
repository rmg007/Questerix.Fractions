## Purpose

Your child will play **Questerix Fractions**, an educational game about fractions (halves, thirds, fourths). This informal playtest helps us observe whether children can use the game and enjoy learning with it. We are **not** conducting a formal research study yet — this is early feedback to catch any obvious problems before a larger test in summer 2026.

---

## What Your Child Will Do

- Play the game for **one session (~15 minutes)** on a tablet or computer
- Answer **5–10 questions** about halves, thirds, and fourths using drag-and-drop interactions
- No pen-and-paper testing today (that comes in Phase 3)

---

## Privacy & Data

- We will **not** collect your child's real name, address, phone number, or email
- Your child chooses a display name (e.g., "Alex")
- Game data (questions answered, time taken, correct/incorrect) is stored locally on the device and exported to a password-protected file on our computer
- **Data will not be shared** with schools, teachers, or third parties
- After the playtest, your data will be deleted unless you give permission to keep it for longer-term validation (optional, see below)

---

## Benefits & Risks

**Benefits:**

- Your child gets to play an early version of an educational game
- Your feedback helps us make the game better

**Risks:**

- Minimal. The game uses standard web technologies (no tracking, no ads, no in-app purchases)
- If your child finds the game frustrating, they can stop at any time with no penalty
