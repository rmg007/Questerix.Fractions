## What we're asking

Your child is invited to play a browser-based math game for **three short sessions** (~15 minutes each) over about two weeks. Before and after the sessions, your child will complete one brief paper activity (~10 minutes) to help us understand whether the game helped.

Participation is entirely voluntary. Your child may stop at any time.

---

## What we collect

- **In the app:** A display name your child chooses (no real names), and a log of which questions were answered correctly or incorrectly. This log stays on the device — it never leaves.
- **On paper:** The pre- and post-session activity sheets, kept in a locked folder.
- **Observer notes:** Written notes about what the child did during play (no audio or video recording).

We do **not** collect: real names, school information, photos, audio, video, or any data tied to a real identity.

---

## How data is used

Data is used only to decide whether the mechanic teaches fraction concepts. Results are analyzed in aggregate and anonymized. This is a small personal pilot — results will not be published in academic journals or used in marketing without a separate consent process.

---

## How to withdraw

You or your child may withdraw at any time without explanation. Contact [email/GitHub link]. Data collected up to that point will be deleted unless you indicate otherwise.
