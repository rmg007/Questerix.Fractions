## Title

**Questerix Fractions**

---

## One-Paragraph Description

Learn fractions through magnetic dragging. Questerix Fractions is a free,
no-signup math app for children ages 5–8. Students drag, split, and match
fraction shapes to build a real understanding of halves, thirds, and fourths —
before they ever see a written symbol. Everything runs in the browser; nothing
leaves the device.

---

## How to Play (3 bullets)

- **Pick a level.** Start at Level 1 and unlock the next level when you're ready.
- **Play.** Drag the pieces where they belong. The app gives hints if you get stuck.
- **No signup.** No account, no email, no password. Just open the app and play.
