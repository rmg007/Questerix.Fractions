## Administrator Instructions (for the Observer)

### Before the Session

1. **Prepare the device:**
   - Open the app in the correct game level (Session 1 = Level 1; Session 2 = Level 2, etc.)
   - Ensure device battery is ≥ 50%
   - Mute notifications but leave audio ON for the game
   - Close all other apps

2. **Prepare the tester:**
   - Greet the child warmly
   - Explain: "Today you get to play a game about fractions. No right or wrong answers—just play and have fun!"
   - Ask: "What's your display name for the game?" (if not already provided)
   - **Do NOT explain the game mechanics** — let them discover

3. **Set expectations:**
   - Duration: "We'll play for about 15 minutes"
   - Breaks: "If you need a break, just let me know"
   - Pausing: "It's totally okay to pause if you're confused"
   - Ending: "When time is up, we'll talk about what you saw"

### During the Session

**Your role:** Observe silently. Take notes. **Do NOT give hints or explanations.**

- Watch for moments of **clarity** (e.g., "Oh, I see now!"), **confusion** (head tilt, hesitation), **joy** (smile, laugh), or **frustration** (sigh, restart button mashing)
- Note the **exact time** of any notable moment (see Notable Moments section)
- If the tester asks a question, deflect with: "What do you think?" or "Try it and see"
- If a technical glitch occurs, note it immediately (see Technical Glitches section)
- If the tester wants to withdraw, stop immediately and note "withdrew at [time]"

**Do NOT:**
- Explain fractions, equal parts, or game rules
- Point at the screen or use hand gestures
- Check your phone or distract the tester
- Praise or criticize specific answers (say "Good effort" regardless)

### After the Session

1. On the device, tap Settings (gear icon, top-right of the menu) → **"Export My Backup"**
2. Save the exported file as: `session-<number>-<pseudonym>-<date>.json`
3. Have the tester take a 5-min break
4. Ask the debrief questions (see Debrief section below)
5. Review your notes and flag any technical issues
