- Add file-level gates: `src/components/**` ≥40%, `src/lib/**` ≥40%, `src/curriculum/**` ≥60%
- Rationale: Enforce incremental progress; 40% is achievable (happy path + 1 edge case per file); 60% for curriculum (data-heavy, high ROI)
